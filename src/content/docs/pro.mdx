

# Pro Overview

Trio Agent Pro is a subscription service that gives users several perks on top of the core free product:

- **AI credits**: Monthly credits for access to leading AI models.
- **Pro modes**: Designed to maximize your credits and build faster.
- **Office hours**: Direct access to the creators during office hours.
- **Learning content**: Access exclusive content to level up your AI coding skills.

You can subscribe to Trio Agent Pro on the pricing page.
